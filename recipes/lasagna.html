Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is one of my personal favorite dishes, the mix of cheese, meats and breading make it appetizing to even the most picky of eaters.

Ingredients

  • Favorite Pasta Sauce
  • Noodles
  • four cheeses

Directions

  1. Place layer of noodle on glass plate
  2. Spread a generous amount of sauce across noodles
  3. Cover ground beef as the next layer
  4. Cover the beeg with a layer of feta cheese
  5. Repeat previous steps for 3 layers
