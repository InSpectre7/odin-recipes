Tacos Recipe

Description

Tacos are an amazing item, almost revolutionary in its impact on food culture. Here's how to make the best tacos for your next family party.

Ingredients

For this recipe you will only need a few ingredients to make the best tacos.

  • 1 pounds of chicken breast
  • 1 pound of carne asada
  • 2-4 lemons
  • plenty of limes
  • cilantro
  • 2 whole white onion
  • 1 pack of shredded cabbage
  • 2 tablespoons of meat seasoning
  • 1 pack of corn tortillas

Preparation

  • Finely chop all your onions into small squares. This will be used for your tacos at the end and some for cooking. Do the same with your cilantro.
  • slice limes into halves or quarters
  • season carne asada meat with lemon and meat seasoning for 5-10 minutes

Directions

  1. Lightly salt a large pan and fill with water, enough for your chicken breast to be submerged in water. On medium high heat, place your chicken breast and cover with a lid to poach the chicken. Once chicken has been fully cooked after 12-15 minutes remove and beging to shred chicken with a fork into small pieces
  2. On a medium high heat pan, place seasoned carne asada meat on let it cook for 5 minutes before turning over. Once turned over cook for an additional 3 minutes or until desired rarity. Once done, finely chop carne asada into small bits.
  3. Combine your carne asada meat with cilantro and onion and serve on tortilla
  4. combine your pulled poached chicken breast with cabbage and cheese and serve on tortilla
